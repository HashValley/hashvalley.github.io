About us

Hash Valley was born during the first Cardano incentivised testnet and it's been online since then.

Our server are hosted in a certified Tier 3 datacenter with high stability and redundancy, thanks to this and to our experience we can bring optimal performance for our pool and your staked funds.

How to stake

To stake your funds to our pool and receive the rewards you need to install a Cardano wallet, you can choose between Daedalus and Yoroi, you can find the links in the resources menu at the top of this page.

Once installed you can search our ticker HASH in the pool list and click on delegate, you will receive your rewards starting from the next epoch.
